Databases¶

Some of the databases that the group have designed and created.

Melonomics¶

Development of genomic tools in Cucurbits, including the sequencing of the melon genome, and its application for breeding these crop species. Melonomics site

Melogene¶

New genomic tools for Melon (Cucumis melo) breeding: transcriptome, SSR, SNP collections and high throughput genotyping platforms. Melogene site

Cucurbigene¶

New genomic tools for Squash (Cucurbita spp.) breeding: transcriptome, genetic map, SNP collections and high throughput genotyping platforms applied to the competitive development of new cultivars, with improve fruit quality and disease resistance. Cucurbigene site

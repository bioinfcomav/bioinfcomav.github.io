Bioinformatics & genomics¶

The bioinformatic and genomics group of the COMAV institute is devoted to the application of bioinformatic tools and genomic analysis to the study of plant horticultural species. Our main areas of interest are:

  • Study of variation in cultivated and wild populations.

  • Crop history

  • Genomic and transcriptomic analysis

  • Genomic, bioinformatics and genetics education

  • Software and tool development
